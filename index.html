About Shepard

Drawing influences from blues, folk, rock, and jazz, Shepard has established a name for themselves in Victoria with a unique modern rock sound. With blues driven progressions and euphoric vocals, the sound of Shepard will resonate in your soul and leave you breathless.


Their debut EP was recorded and produced by Malcom Owen-Flood (Band of Rascals) and will be released fall of 2019.
